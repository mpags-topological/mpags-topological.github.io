# Course Structure

This Course will consist of 10 one hour lectures. These will be hosted on Microsoft Teams on Mondays 10-12, beginning 2nd February for 5 weeks.

There will be 5 problem sheets (1 per week) which are designed to build on what is shown in the lectures. In order to pass this course a clear attempt must be made for all questions. Problem sheets will be issued by email and will be due a week after they are set. 

If there are any issues or questions regarding the course structure or assessment, please don't hesitate to contact us.
